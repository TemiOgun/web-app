About Us

This is Rock for Dimes

Rock for Dimes is an annual fundraising battle of the bands. Amateur rock bands, made up of members from the corporate and music industry, compete in this friendly fundraiser for the title of their city's best corporate band and showcase their talents in front of a roster of Canadian music industry professionals.

Beginning with a single event in Toronto in 2004, Rock for Dimes is now March of Dimes Canada's national fundraiser, with events in fifteen cities across Canada. Funds raised support the ongoing efforts of March of Dimes Canada.

Who Are We?

We are the March of Dimes Canada

March of Dimes Canada (MODC) is a community-based rehabilitation and advocacy charity for people with physical disabilities. MODC provides a wide range of important programs and services, from physical rehabilitation to peer support networks, from recreation activities to advocacy, to enhance the independence, personal empowerment and community participation of people with disabilities.

Our Projects

Our proceeds from Rock for Dime goes towards March of Dimes Canada's charity projects

Stroke Recovery Canada®

Stroke Recovery Canada® offers post-recovery support, education and programs for stroke survivors, their families and health care providers. Through its work with local peer support groups across the country, Stroke Recovery Canada provides Canadians with the resources needed to help stroke survivors “Survive and Thrive” after a stroke.

Conductive Education® Program

Conductive Education® combines physical rehabilitation and education to help children and adults with motor-neuro disabilities improve their physical mobility.

Campers Helping Campers

Campers Helping Campers is an annual fund raising program that takes place from May-October. Funds raised support participants in March of Dimes Canada’s Outdoor Recreation Programs. Funds are used to subsidize the costs associated with supporting a person who lives with a disability so they can enjoy a summer camping experience.
